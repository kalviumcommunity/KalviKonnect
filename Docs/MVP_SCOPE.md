# 📦 MVP Scope Bucket List

## 🟢 1. MVP Bucket | **"The Must-Haves"**
*These features are critical for solve the problem of information fragmentation.*

- **Feature 1: User Auth (JWT)** — *Justification:* Necessary to keep the community exclusive to Kalvium students and staff (Kalvium email only).
- **Feature 2: Academic Notes Module** — *Justification:* Solves the most immediate pain of missing files from WhatsApp threads.
- **Feature 3: Placement Experience Feed** — *Justification:* Captures high-value "tribal knowledge" before it's lost when seniors graduate.
- **Feature 4: Official Announcements** — *Justification:* Provides a single source of truth for campus managers that can't be muted or drowned out.

---

## 🟡 2. Post-MVP Bucket | **"The Nice-to-Haves"**
*These will be added once the core habit of using Kalvi Connect is formed.*

- **Feature 1: Resource Upvoting/Ratings** — Provides a quality filter for notes and experiences.
- **Feature 2: Mentor Calendars** — Booking 1:1 sessions with seniors for mock interviews.
- **Feature 3: Job/Internship Tags** — Directly linking hackathons to potential hiring opportunities.

---

## 🔴 3. Cut Bucket | **"Out of Scope"**
*Deliberate decisions to stay focused and ship on time.*

- **Feature 1: Real-time Chat (DM)** — *Reason:* WhatsApp and Discord already handle this; we are a "Knowledge Hub," not a "Chat App."
- **Feature 2: Native Mobile App** — *Reason:* Web-first allows for faster iteration and zero-install friction for desktop-based students.
- **Feature 3: Video Hosting** — *Reason:* Storing 1GB+ files is expensive and complex; we will only allow embedding (YouTube/Vimeo) links.
- **Feature 4: Advanced Gamification** — *Reason:* Badges and scores can lead to low-quality spam; prioritize quality content first.

# 👥 User Personas (LU 4)

## 1. Persona Cards

### 👨‍🎓 **Abhishek | The Junior / Student**
- **Role:** 1st/2nd Year Student (LPU Branch)
- **Primary Goal:** To find high-quality notes and hackathon teams to survive and thrive in a fast-paced academic environment.
- **Top 2 Needs:**
    1.  Clean, searchable semester notes (Digital and Handwritten). 
    2.  Transparency into "Hackathon Teaming" to avoid working with the same 3 people every time.
- **What they do NOT care about:** Managing site-wide announcements or long-term placement legacy (yet).
- **Features that serve them:** Notes & Resources, Hackathons Module, Discussion Forum.

---

### 👩‍💼 **Meghana | The Senior / Mentor**
- **Role:** 4th Year Student (Placed at Google)
- **Primary Goal:** To document her interview experiences so her juniors don't "go in blind" like she did.
- **Top 2 Needs:**
    1.  A structured template to post interview rounds (Round 1, Round 2, Round 3 details).
    2.  The ability to build a "Mentor Profile" to guide students centrally.
- **What they do NOT care about:** Basic first-semester notes or university-wide administrative schedules.
- **Features that serve them:** Placement Experiences, Discussions, Mention/Notify Juniors.

---

### 🏫 **Mr. Vinayak | The Campus Manager**
- **Role:** Administrator / Point of Contact
- **Primary Goal:** To ensure every student sees critical hackathon or placement "Alerts" without them being muted in a WhatsApp group.
- **Top 2 Needs:**
    1.  A "Sticky" announcement feature that stays at the top of the student dashboard.
    2.  Analytics on who has viewed the official notices.
- **What they do NOT care about:** Technical notes or peer-to-peer discussions on code bugs.
- **Features that serve them:** Announcements Module (Creation only).
